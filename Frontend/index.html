Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

How does the AI matching system work?

Our AI analyzes your skills, career goals, and location to match you with the most suitable mentors and opportunities in your area. The system prioritizes local connections to reduce travel costs and build stronger community networks.

What is the 500 FCFA annual fee for?

The annual fee gives you unlimited access to our platform, AI matching services, and the ability to connect with mentors and organizations. You pay separately for individual services directly to the providers.

How much do mentors earn from sessions?

Mentors keep 92% of their session fees, with only 8% going to platform commission. This ensures fair compensation for quality mentorship while maintaining platform sustainability.

Is EmpowerU available outside Cameroon?

We're starting with a strong local foundation in Cameroon, particularly in Buea and Douala. Our expansion plan includes other African countries and eventually global reach while maintaining our local-first approach.
